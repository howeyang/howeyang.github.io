A Little About Me

Hi, my name is Howe Yang. I am an aspiring game developer and animator.
I love sketching out my imagination, making cool visuals, learning from different systems and tinkering with other people's work. I am always trying to be professional and push myself to the extent of what I know and then try to fill in any missing pieces. I enjoy working with the whole pipeline of a system so that I can work with multiple components and people.

My passion for games and design started from early exposure to books, animations and games. Powered by an active imagination, I found myself pursuing this line of work with my end goal of making things that can inspire the next generation.
