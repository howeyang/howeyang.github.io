    A Little About Me

    Hi, my name is Howe Yang. I am an aspiring Canadian developer with a passion for games.
    I love sketching out my imagination, making cool visuals, learning from different systems and tinkering with other people's work. I always try to maintain a professional but fun relationship with my co-workers. If I ever find myself with gaps in knowledge, I like to reach out to others around me or research the topic to figure it out. I enjoy working with the whole pipeline of a system so that I can work with multiple components and people!

    My passion for games and coding started from early exposure to books, animations and games. Powered by an active imagination, I found myself pursuing this line of work with my end goal of making things that can inspire the next generation.
