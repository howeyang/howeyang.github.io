A Little About Me

Hi, my name is Howe Yang. I am an aspiring game developer and animator.
I love sketching out my imagination, storyboarding ideas, making games, building models and appreciating other people's work. I am always trying to learn new things and the whole pipeline of a system, with the end goal of making things that can inspire the next generation.
